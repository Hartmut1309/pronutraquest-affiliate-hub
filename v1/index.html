Compliance

The rules that keep everyone's account alive

We run long-term offers in regulated health categories. These rules are enforced without exceptions — violations mean immediate, permanent blacklisting across all our offers.

  1. No spam. Ever. Email traffic must be CAN-SPAM / GDPR compliant, with working unsubscribe and honest sender identity.
  2. No deceptive claims. Nothing misleading, untruthful or unsubstantiated. Use the claims in our official materials — do not invent cure promises, disease claims or guaranteed results.
  3. No fake authority. No fabricated testimonials, no before/after photos without written permission, no deepfakes or celebrity endorsements, no fake news formats.
  4. Don't impersonate the brand. No brand-bidding on our product names in paid search without written approval, and never present yourself as the product owner.
  5. Send traffic to our funnels. No direct-linking to checkout that bypasses the offer presentation, and no selling on Amazon, eBay or other retail platforms.
  6. Disclose your affiliation. FTC/consumer-law compliant disclosure on review pages, ads and emails, in the local language of your traffic.
  7. Respect the customer. No "scam"-style negative advertising against our or competitor products, and no retargeting scraped from our pixels.
  8. When unsure, ask first. One email to support beats a burned account. Gray-area channels (Telegram blasts, incentivized traffic, adult) need written approval.
